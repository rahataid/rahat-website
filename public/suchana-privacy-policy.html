Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Rumsan Associates ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Suchana App mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

b. Camera Data

The App requests access to your device camera to allow you to take photos for creating and submitting alerts. Photos are captured only when you actively use the alert feature. We do not access your camera in the background.

c. NFC Data

The App uses your device's NFC (Near Field Communication) capability to scan NFC tags. NFC data is read only when you actively initiate a scan within the App.

d. Location Data

The App collects your device location data to provide location-relevant services and to associate alerts with geographic information. Location data is collected only while you are using the App.

e. Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for the purpose of app functionality and troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Process and deliver alerts with associated photos and location data
  • Read NFC tag data for in-app functionality
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Communicate with you regarding your account or the App

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on our own servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We use the following third-party services solely for app functionality:

  • Mapbox - for map and location services. Mapbox may receive anonymized location data to render maps. See Mapbox Privacy Policy.
  • Expo - for app build and update services. See Expo Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
